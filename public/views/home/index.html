ABOUT US

SMU Study Buddy was founded in the midst of the COVID-19 pandemic when regulations kicked in where the maximum group of 2 is allowed to be together at any time.

We hope that this platform helps students to Reach Out to a New Buddy to study together. As we believe that studying together physically may boost productivity and help students be at the peak of their performance.
